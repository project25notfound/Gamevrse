🎯 How to Play Blackjack Roulette

🏆 Goal: Be the last player alive by surviving blackjack rounds and deadly roulette spins.

📋 Game Flow

  • Round Start: Host starts the round, all players get 2 cards
  • Blackjack Phase: Players take turns hitting or standing (15s each)
  • Roulette Phase: Chosen player faces elimination
  • Repeat: Continue until only one player remains

🃏 Blackjack Phase

  • Hit → Draw another card (risk busting over 21)
  • Stand → Keep your current hand value
  • Bust → Go over 21 (automatically at risk for roulette)
  • Goal: Get close to 21 without going over

🎰 Roulette Selection

🎯 Standard Mode (Default):
  • All Busted: Random busted player chosen
  • Mixed Results: Busted players chosen first
  • No Busts: Player with lowest hand value chosen
  • Ties: Random selection among tied players
⚖️ Lowest-Hand Mode (Optional):
  • Always: Player with lowest hand value chosen
  • Bust Status: Ignored for roulette selection
  • Ties: Random selection among tied players
  • Note: Busting still matters for blackjack rules
  • Final 2 Exception: If one player busts, they go to roulette (overrides mode)

⚡ Roulette Choices (30s to decide)

🎲 Risk Level:
  • Normal: Roll 1-6, eliminated on 1-2 (33% death chance)
  • 🃏 Second Chance Card: Use your card to avoid elimination
    • One-time use per game (cannot be replenished)
    • Guarantees survival regardless of dice roll
    • Card is consumed immediately after use
⏱️ Timing Choice:
  • Pull Now: Immediate trigger pull
  • Spin Chamber: 3-second chamber spinning animation first

🃏 Second Chance Card System

  • Starting Cards: Every player begins each game with 1 Second Chance Card
  • How to Use: Choose "Second Chance Card" during roulette selection
  • Effect: Guarantees survival - you cannot be eliminated that round
  • Limitations: One-time use per game, consumed immediately after use
  • Visual: 🃏 icon appears on your player card when available
  • Strategy: Decide when to use - save for later rounds or use early for safety

⚔️ Final 2 Showdown

  • Special Atmosphere: Dramatic lighting and sound effects
  • Second Chance Cards: Still available if players have unused cards
  • Slower Animations: Increased tension with longer reveals
  • Winner Reveal: Dramatic pause before victory announcement

👁️ Spectator Features

  • Mid-Game Joining: New players become spectators automatically
  • Reactions: Send emoji reactions during gameplay
  • Join Next Game: Button to rejoin as player for next round
  • Full Visibility: See all game logs and round summaries

🤖 Practice Mode

  • Difficulty Levels: Easy, Normal, Hard bot intelligence
  • Safe Mode: Reduced sound effects and animations
  • No Waiting: Bots are auto-ready for instant gameplay

👑 Host Controls

  • Start Rounds: Control when games begin
  • Elimination Mode: Choose Standard or Lowest-Hand mode (lobby only)
  • Kick Players: Remove disruptive players (lobby only)
  • Ban System: Kicked players cannot rejoin the same room
  • Room Management: Full control over room settings
  • Host Transfer: Automatic if host leaves
